The BeaujoLoop Experience

BeaujoLoop provides a premium vintage scooter concierge service, removing the logistical barriers so you can savor the romance of Beaujolais. Our service includes luggage transfers, GPS-guided routes through scenic D-roads, and VIP winery tastings—all with a fleet of fully restored Italian Vespas in our signature Beaujolais pastel colors.
